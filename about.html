ABOUT US

Dera is a Nigerian lady, proud of her heritage and unwilling to compromise on her true identity.

"Over the years, I have learnt that true beauty comes from loving and expressing ourselves as we are, and not from struggling towards perfection. Confidence is where true beauty starts.

After a year of intensive research in the cosmetic industry in the UK, I was inspired to create an inclusive makeup line that remedies fundamental inadequacies for women who look like me; this is why I decided through Dera's artistry, to make quality products available that would suit simmilar skin tones and be easily accessible across Europe and africa.

I deeply believe that every colored woman should be able to find the right makeup that matches her skin tone and projects her personaal style, without having to compromise, sacrifice quality, lose time mixing and matching, or break the bank.

Dera's Artistry makeup line is designed to accentuate your natural beauty, not conceal it; and I am committed to helping every colored woman feel confident and beautiful. I will forever stand by those values no matter what".

With Dera's Artistry, there are no more excuses preventing you from radiating; it is time to find your perfect match!
